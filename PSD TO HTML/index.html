HOW IT WORK

IT'S A FUNNEL,NOT ROCKET SCIENCE

Funnel are way to visually measure how customers move through any series of events. Creating a funnel in Mixpanel is as simple as choosing an event

Other analytics tools ask you to set variables of rules,use fancy syntax,or fill in complicate forms.Just tell Mixpanel each event you want to track and you're done.

SEE HOW FAST PEOPLE WILL CONVERT,YOU'RE IN CONTROL

Funnels give you the control to understand whether people are completing an important part of your application in time you expect them to.For example you can understand if people are not uploading a picture as soon as they sign up-we'll help you understand if people are converting immediately.Funnels give you control to really understand when,not just where you're losing users
